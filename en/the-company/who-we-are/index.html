Who We Are

We live in a mobile world. We know things and new people, we access services, solve problems, we search for information on our smartphones or tablets. This world has inspired Take’s creation, in 1999, and it is in this world where we thrive. Mobility is in our DNA.

Since the first day, we’ve been seeking to improve people’s experience with their cell phones, helping them to overcome physical barriers and to achieve even more, wherever they are.

People want to talk to companies in the same way they talk to each other: via messages! We will make this vision come true.
Roberto Oliveira, Founder and CEO

We will become an important change instrument through which the digital world passes by: mobile and desktop web have merged. With our expertise in messaging, we want to help organizations to adapt and to thrive in this new digital age, supporting them in the development of mobile channels for communication and service, content and sales delivery via messages exchange.

Our Values

Take is made of people and for people. Our relations are transparent and humanized. Each customer, each partner and each Take.Be is valued and respected in his/her individuality and is present in each bit of our company.

Take.Higher We are not afraid of dreaming big, for we know we have talent and grit to make incredible things come true!

Take.Simple We keep our focus on what is essential. Less is more.

Take.Charge We invest on responsible professionals and on those who do not fear to dare and transform the world around them with great expertise and creativity!

Take.Team We know that what makes an amazing team is the collaboration of amazing people. Each Take.Ser does his part to ensure the victory of the team!

Take.Excellence With our geek spirit and our eagerness to learn and to grow, we seek to always be one step ahead and to overcome the most demanding standards.
